    Located in the heart of Toronto, Fitty focuses on providing healthy food at a deliciously affordable grasp

    Fitty Health Restaurant was established in 2016 as a healthy alternative to fine dining. Our Mission is to make great food accessable, accompanied by quality culture and a classic but classy dining experience.

    Our Restaurant is located in the heart of downtown Toronto just across from Queen's Park subway station. All you have to do intersection walk across the intersection and your here!

    The Menu at Fitty consists of quality, fresh foods and smoothies. Each item made with love and health in mind to provide a deliciously guilt free meal. The selection is expansive, from salads to bowls and even your favourate steak dinner, Fitty is sure to appease your taste buds
